## Overview

This document outlines the process for requesting an exam voucher for the AI-900: Azure AI Fundamentals certification exam.

## Voucher Request Process

### Step 1: Confirm Eligibility

Before requesting a voucher, ensure you have:

- [ ] Completed all required course modules (251-264)
- [ ] Passed all practice exams with a score of 70% or higher
- [ ] Completed all required POC badge submissions
- [ ] Completed all required GLAB submissions

### Step 2: Submit Your Voucher Request

1. Contact your instructor (Alexandros Karales - akarales@perscholas.org) to confirm your eligibility.
2. Your instructor will verify your completion status in Canvas.
3. Once verified, a voucher code will be provided to you.

### Step 3: Schedule Your Exam

1. Visit the [Pearson VUE scheduling page](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/schedule-through-pearson-vue?examUid=exam.AI-900&examUrl=https%3A%2F%2Flearn.microsoft.com%2Fcredentials%2Fcertifications) or [Certiport](https://certiport.pearsonvue.com/Certifications/Microsoft/Schedule).
2. Sign in with your **personal Microsoft account** (not organizational).
3. Select the AI-900 exam.
4. Apply your voucher code during checkout.
5. Choose your preferred date, time, and testing location (in-person or online).

### Step 4: Prepare for Exam Day

- Arrive 15 minutes early if testing in person.
- Ensure your ID is valid and matches your registration name.
- For online proctored exams, test your system in advance using the Pearson VUE system check tool.

---

## Important Notes

* Voucher codes have expiration dates. Schedule your exam promptly after receiving your voucher.
* If the AI-900 has been retired and replaced by AI-901, confirm with your instructor which exam to schedule.
* Register with a **personal MSA account**, not an organizational account.
