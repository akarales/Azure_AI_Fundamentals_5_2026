## **Overview:**

As part of the **Generative AI Concepts - Certifications** learning path, your progress will be tracked through individual badges.

This lab is a **required, graded assessment** that supports your progress in the *Azure AI Fundamentals Certification* learning path. Your task is to demonstrate successful completion of the **"[Fundamentals of Generative AI](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/modules/fundamentals-generative-ai/)"** module on Microsoft Learn by submitting a screenshot of the earned badge.

## **Instructions:**

Follow these steps to complete the submission:

1. **Log into Microsoft Learn:**
   1. Visit [Microsoft Learn](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/) and log in using your account.

2. **Access Your Badge:**
   1. Navigate to your profile and locate the badge associated with this assignment.

3. **Take a Screenshot:**
   1. Capture a clear screenshot that includes:
      1. The badge name.
      2. Your name.
      3. Completion date (if visible).

## **Note:**

* Ensure the screenshot is clear and legible.
* Contact your instructor if you encounter any issues accessing or capturing your badge.
