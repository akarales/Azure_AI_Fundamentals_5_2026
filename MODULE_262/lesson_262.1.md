## Section 1: What is NLP?

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a branch of artificial intelligence that focuses on enabling machines to understand, interpret, and generate human language. NLP bridges the gap between human communication and computer understanding.

### Key NLP Tasks

- **Key Phrase Extraction:** Identifying the main points or topics in a block of text.
- **Entity Recognition:** Detecting and classifying named entities (people, places, organizations, dates) in text.
- **Sentiment Analysis:** Determining whether text expresses positive, negative, or neutral sentiment.
- **Language Detection:** Identifying the language of a given text.
- **Text Summarization:** Condensing long text into shorter summaries while preserving key information.

---

## Section 2: Text Analytics

Text analytics is the process of deriving insights from unstructured text data. Azure AI Language provides several text analytics capabilities:

- **Key Phrase Extraction:** Automatically extracts the most important phrases from text.
- **Named Entity Recognition (NER):** Identifies entities such as people, locations, organizations, and quantities.
- **Sentiment Analysis:** Analyzes text to determine overall sentiment with confidence scores.
- **Opinion Mining:** Identifies specific aspects and the sentiments expressed about them.

---

## Section 3: Language Modeling

Language models are AI models trained on large amounts of text data to understand and generate human language. They power many NLP applications including:

- Chatbots and virtual assistants
- Machine translation
- Text generation and summarization
- Question answering

---

## Section 4: Azure AI Language Service

Azure AI Language is a cloud-based service that provides NLP features for text analysis.

### Key Capabilities

- **Text Analytics:** Sentiment analysis, key phrase extraction, entity recognition, language detection.
- **Conversational Language Understanding (CLU):** Build custom language models to understand user intents and entities in conversational scenarios.
- **Question Answering:** Create knowledge bases and chatbots that can answer questions based on your content.
- **Text Summarization:** Generate concise summaries of documents and conversations.

**Reference:** [Microsoft Learn - Analyze Text with AI Language](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/modules/analyze-text-ai-language/)
